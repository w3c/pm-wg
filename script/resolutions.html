Publishing Maintenance Working Group — Meeting Resolutions

These are the formal resolutions accepted by the Publishing Maintenance Working Group on WG calls or meetings. For further details on the Working Group, its resources, joining instructions, etc., see the Working Group Home page. See also the separate index page for the list of all meeting minutes.

  • Resolutions in 2025

    • Publish the FPWDs of EPUB 3.4, EPUB Reading Systems 3.4, and EPUB Accessibility 1.1.1, with the shortnames epub-34, epub-rs-34, and epub-a11y-111 (Thu Mar 13 2025)
    • Request new URL redirects for w3.org/TR/epub, w3.org/TR/epub3 and w3.org/TR/epub-3 to always point to the latest recommendation status version of the EPUB3 specification (Thu Mar 13 2025)
    • Merge PR 2686. (Thu Feb 27 2025)
    • Merge PR 2685, add dark mode to EPUB 3.4 drafts. (Thu Feb 27 2025)
    • The working group will keep the name Publishing Maintenance Working Group (Fri Feb 07 2025)

  • Resolutions in 2024

    • Republish EPUB 3.3, EPUB Reading Systems 3.3, and EPUB Accessibility 1.1 as Candidate Amendments (Fri Oct 04 2024)

  • Resolutions in 2023

    • WG agrees to explore publishing to ISO when we have a new version of EPUB, EPUB Reading Systems, or EPUB Accessibility (Tue Sep 12 2023)
    • WG agrees to publish the 3 recs as ISO standards under PAS process once the European Process is completed (Tue Sep 12 2023)
    • Merge PR 2572 (Mon Sep 11 2023)
    • Merge PR 2571, publish a new note for the Exemption property on oct 3, short name will be "epub-a11y-exemption" (Mon Sep 11 2023)
